Assay Notes

For iCell Cardiomyocytes, both nuclei staining and mitochondrial integrity assays are now in the nuclei staining assay group.

For Human Umbilical Vein Endothelial Cells and iCell Endothelial Cells, the Cyto assay group includes three assays: nuclei staining, mitochondrial integrity, and cell viability.

For iCell Neurons, the neurons assay group includes three assays: nuclei staining, mito tracker, and cell viability.
